Our Services

What Services Do We Provide For You?

Joint Replacement Surgery

Restore mobility and relieve chronic joint pain with advanced joint replacement procedures. Our expert orthopedic surgeons specialize in knee, hip, and shoulder replacements using modern techniques for faster recovery and long-lasting results.

Spine Disorders

Specialized diagnosis and treatment for back pain, slipped discs, and spinal deformities. Our advanced care helps restore mobility, reduce pain, and improve overall spinal health.

Pediatric Orthopedics

Expert care for bone, joint, and growth-related conditions in children. We provide gentle, specialized treatments to ensure healthy development and long-term mobility.

Sports Related Injury

Dedicated treatment for sprains, fractures, ligament tears, and overuse injuries. Our team ensures safe recovery and helps you return to sports and daily activities with confidence.

X-Ray

Quick and accurate digital imaging services to diagnose fractures, joint issues, and other conditions. Our on-site X-ray facility ensures fast results for timely treatment.

Physiotherapy

Personalized rehabilitation programs to restore strength, flexibility, and mobility. Our expert physiotherapists help in faster recovery from injuries, surgeries, and chronic pain.
